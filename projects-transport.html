Sydney Transport

The Sydney Transport app is a conceptual mobile application. It allows people using the Sydney transport system to plan their trips and manage their tickets all in once place.
Problems and key drivers:

1) Seperate apps
Currently, "TripView" is the app that customers use to find out about transport times and routes. A seperate app called "Opal Travel" allows users to manage their Opal Card which is the ticketing system that allows customers to use the transport.
2) Outdated aesthetic:
The current TripView app has an outdated aesthetic that looks unpleasant compared to modern day user interfaces. It is also vastly inconsistent with the Opal Travel UI.
